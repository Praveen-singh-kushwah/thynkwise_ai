Knowledge Base

Frequently asked questions

What CRM systems does ThynkWISE implement?▾

Salesforce (Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, Marketing Cloud), Microsoft Dynamics 365, Apollo.io for B2B outreach, and HubSpot — all four platforms. Salesforce and Dynamics are delivered through the Nsquare certified partnership.

What is Apollo.io and why implement it?▾

Apollo.io combines B2B lead database access, email sequencing, LinkedIn automation, CRM integration, and workflow automation. ThynkWISE implemented it for Clarion Technologies, replacing two disconnected tools, and delivered 5× appointment growth — 2/week to 1/day — within 90 days.

What is ThynkWISE Academy?▾

ThynkWISE Academy (thynkwiseacademy.com) delivers structured sales training, CRM certification programmes, and the GEMS-based methodology in a self-paced, on-demand format. Ideal for teams who need verified credentials alongside implementation. Visit thynkwiseacademy.com to explore courses.

What is a Fractional CSO and who needs one?▾

A Fractional Chief Sales Officer is an experienced sales leader embedded part-time — strategy, team structure, hiring, and execution without a full-time C-suite hire. Best fit: growth-stage companies where the founder is still closing most deals.

How long does a CRM implementation take?▾

Apollo.io and HubSpot: 2–4 weeks. Salesforce or Dynamics 365: 4–12 weeks depending on scope. ThynkWISE provides a detailed project plan after the initial discovery call — no surprises.
