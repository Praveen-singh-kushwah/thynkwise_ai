Before you reach out

Quick answers to the questions we get most often — so your first call is more productive than a Q&A session.

Do you provide local currency quotes with GST?

Always. Every proposal includes local currency pricing with 18% GST line items, HSN codes, and a format compatible with Indian procurement processes. No USD conversion, no hidden forex charges.

How quickly can Thynkwise start?

Managed services onboarding: 5–14 days depending on plan. Cloud migration projects: scoping in week 1, execution from week 3. Free assessment delivered in 3 business days. Emergency support: same day.

Do you work with startups or only enterprise?

Both. Starter managed services suits seed-to-Series A startups. Enterprise plans handle ₹50Cr+ cloud spend. Same team, same SLA, different scale.

Is there a minimum commitment?

For the free assessment and initial consultation — zero. For managed services, the Starter plan has a 3-month minimum; Growth is 6 months. We don't lock clients in beyond what's needed to deliver results.

Which cities can you support on-site?

Mumbai, Bengaluru, Delhi NCR, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune, Ahmedabad, and Kochi. For enterprise clients in other cities, we'll travel. BFSI clients: we accompany RBI inspections regardless of location.

Can you take over from our existing cloud provider?

Yes — this is one of our most common engagements. We do a 2-week parallel monitoring period, then transition management with zero downtime. Previous provider's documentation gaps are handled as part of onboarding.
