Frequently asked questions

What does ThynkWISE do?▾

ThynkWISE is a full-spectrum technology and consulting firm — Sales Consulting (Salesforce, Dynamics 365, Apollo.io, HubSpot, Fractional CSO), AI Avatar as a Service (digital humans in Arabic/English/Hindi), Cloud & Hardware Services (AWS, Azure, GCP, ESDS managed services), and a Partner Ecosystem enabling web development and digital services via vetted specialists.

What is AI Avatar as a Service?▾

ThynkWISE deploys hyper-realistic digital humans in Arabic, English, Hindi and other languages — across web, app, kiosk, WhatsApp, and video. 24/7 availability, zero downtime, no camera crew. Live for AGFund in Arabic and English for donor and applicant guidance.

Does ThynkWISE implement Salesforce and Microsoft Dynamics 365?▾

Yes — through the Nsquare certified partnership. Salesforce (Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, Marketing Cloud) and Microsoft Dynamics 365 (Sales, Customer Insights, Power Platform). Enterprise-grade deployment, data migration, and ongoing support across India and the Middle East.

What results has ThynkWISE delivered for clients?▾

5× appointment growth for Clarion Technologies (Apollo.io, 90 days). 150% brand awareness for DataFortune and SCISPL. 40% lead growth for DataFortune. 4× website traffic for Barcode Gulf in UAE/KSA. 28% average cloud cost reduction for managed services clients.

Where does ThynkWISE operate?▾

Headquartered in Pune, India. Active presence across Mumbai, Delhi, Ahmedabad, Chennai, Bengaluru, and Kochi. Serving clients globally in USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Netherlands, UAE, and Saudi Arabia.
